Aftervibe

January 2008 - June 2008 (defunct)

Aftervibe was a startup I co-founded with two friends in January 2008 as a full-time venture. Aftervibe was a storytelling platform that synchronized with Facebook Events, and had plans for additional event service integration. It consisted of a standalone website and a Facebook app, both which allowed you to collaboratively write stories about parties with your friends.

I did all of the graphical work on Aftervibe, including the logo, business cards, and interfaces for all pages on the site and facebook app. I then implemented these designs with semantic HTML, CSS, sprites, and FBML. Aftervibe was a Ruby on Rails app.

Unfortunately Aftervibe is no longer live. However, you can look through these screenshots and imagine what it was like back then... back then... back then... back then...
