Def

Since July 2007

Def locates press-quality album artwork via a simple search interface. It is the product of my long time obsession with high resolution album artwork. Def has a small userbase consisting of me and a handful of users as hungry for high res album art as I am.

Def was developed solely by me in PHP5, and the front end consists of a bit of CSS and vanilla Javascript.
